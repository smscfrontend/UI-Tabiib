How is TABIIB Plus different from other health cards?

TABIIB Plus is the only health card that is available in physical as well as digital format. It also helps patients book appointments and avail offers from the same account. This makes it different from the rest of the health cards like Sama, Al Reaaiaa etc.

It is very easy to get started with TABIIB Plus. Just visit www.tabiib.com/login in case you are already a member. Add up to 5 family members in your card. If you are a new user you can click on www.tabiib.com/register and we will get back to you at the earliest.

About TABIIB PLUS

TABIIB Plus is a discount health card available for residents, citizens and visitors. Members can get instant discounts on medical services such as appointments, labs, medicines and more. TABIIB Plus is the only health card that is available in physical and digital formats, across Bahrain. Individuals and family members can subscribe to this card and avail its benefits.
